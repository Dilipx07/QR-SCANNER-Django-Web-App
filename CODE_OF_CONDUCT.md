# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep project discussions respectful, constructive, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive or hostile comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior is harmful to the project community.
